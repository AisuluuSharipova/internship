Undergraduate Program Admission

Program Details:

Duration: 4 years (5 years for distance learning)

Language of instruction: English

Tuition Fee: $3,500 per year

Golden Certificate Scholarship

Students with a Golden Certificate from the General Republican Testing (GRT) receive 100% tuition fee waiver.

The full scholarship covers the entire $3,500 tuition fee for each academic year.

Eligibility: Applicants must present the original Golden Certificate during the admission process.

Benefits: In addition to the full tuition waiver, Golden Certificate holders receive priority in dormitory allocation and academic counseling.

Admission Requirements:

AIU does not have entrance exams, but an internal English language test is conducted. Detailed information about the test is available here. This test helps determine students' English proficiency level and place them in appropriate levels.

Admission of applicants is based on achieving the threshold score in the main test of the General Republican Testing (GRT).

For admission to the Faculty of Engineering and Informatics at AIU, in addition to the main GRT test, it is recommended to choose additional subjects - Physics and Mathematics with scores of 60 and above.

List of Required Documents for Admission:

  1. State-issued document of secondary general or secondary vocational education (Diploma) - original;
  2. Original and copy of GRT certificate;
  3. Copy of passport;
  4. Copy of military registration certificate or military ID (for male applicants);
  5. Six (6) photos sized 3x4;
  6. Initial payment of 30% of the total tuition fee. (Payment is accepted only in national currency at the exchange rate of the National Bank of the Kyrgyz Republic)

Important Notice

For the 2023-2024 academic year, all students must apply to the university through the portal of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Kyrgyz Republic.
