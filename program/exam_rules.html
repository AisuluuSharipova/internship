Examination Rules and Regulations

  • Only students listed in the official "Examination List" will be permitted to take the examination.

  • Students must arrive at the examination room at least ten minutes before the scheduled start time.

  • All candidates must present valid photo identification (signed Photo ID) and place it on their desks. Acceptable forms of ID include: IAAU ID Card, valid Passport, or valid Driver's License.

  • Coats, jackets, backpacks, purses, pencil cases, notes, and books must be deposited in designated areas specified by the examination observers. These items are not permitted at examination desks or tables. If a candidate brings a bag, only the following items may be stored inside it under the candidate's chair: cell phone, wallet, laptop computer, and small electronic devices such as calculators. All pencil cases found on desks will be subject to inspection.

  • The examination observer has the authority to assign seating arrangements to all candidates.

  • Students are strictly prohibited from communicating with one another in any form during the examination. Leaving the examination room unescorted for any reason, including restroom use, is not permitted.

  • Candidates who bring unauthorized materials or electronic devices into the examination room, or who engage in academic dishonesty by giving or receiving assistance, are subject to penalties under the Code of Behaviour on Academic Matters, which may include loss of academic credit and expulsion from the university.

  • Generally, candidates will not be admitted to the examination room more than fifteen minutes after the examination has commenced, nor will they be permitted to leave until at least thirty minutes after the examination has begun.

  • All candidates must remain seated at their desks during the final ten minutes of each examination session.

  • At the conclusion of the examination, all writing must cease immediately. The Chief Presiding Officer may confiscate the papers of candidates who fail to comply with this requirement, and appropriate penalties may be applied.

  • Examination booklets and other materials provided for the examination must not be removed from the examination room without explicit authorization from the Chief Presiding Officer.

Important Notice

THE UNIVERSITY IS NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR PERSONAL PROPERTY LEFT IN EXAMINATION ROOMS

Please ensure you take all personal belongings with you when leaving the examination room.
