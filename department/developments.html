Center for Innovative Development & Research

Goals & Objectives

  • Activation of intellectual, informational, material-technical and financial resources, and providing a platform for students, master's and PhD candidates, and faculty to implement innovative projects.
  • Engaging students, master's and PhD candidates in innovation and entrepreneurial activities.
  • Researching and creating innovations for the university's educational process.
  • Organizing and developing activities for designing high-tech productions based on R&D and innovation.
  • Developing new technologies with subsequent patent protection.
  • Implementing the Center's technologies within the university and partner organizations.
  • Collaborating with other universities and organizations to co-develop innovative technologies.
  • Seeking partners and investors to scale promising projects.
  • Commercialization and monetization of innovative products and solutions.
