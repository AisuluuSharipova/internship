Biography

Esenalieva Nazira Soltonbekovna - Candidate of Economic Sciences, has been serving as a professor at Ala-Too International University since 2014. She worked as the Director of the Science Department at AIU from 2018 to 2023. She has extensive experience in education, science, and management.

In addition to her academic activities, she has actively participated in international educational and social projects. She worked as a national consultant for UN Women Kyrgyzstan, as well as in programs of the European Union, USAID, and HELVETAS Swiss Intercooperation Kyrgyzstan. Within these projects, she focused on sustainable development, digitalization, women's leadership, and inclusive education.

In her welcome speech in her new position, Nazira Soltonbekovna emphasized the importance of developing the academic environment, improving the quality of education, and strengthening the university's scientific potential.
