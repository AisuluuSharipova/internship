Computer Labs


The staff maintain several instructional computing laboratories in B-Block which are open to students in the department. These labs may be reserved by members of the AIU for instructional purposes. When these classes are not used for training, they are opened as regular computing rooms.


Electronics (Lab-1)

Electronics Lab is managed by the Department of Industrial Electronics of the Faculty of New Technologies and is used for laboratory classes and research, personal study.

Specifications
Electronics Lab is equipped with 1 computer, video projector, marker board, soldering equipment, 0-24V power supplies, generators, TTL and CMOS integrated circuits, resistors 1 Ohm >> 2 MOhm, capacitors 0.1 mF >> 470 mF, transistors bc 547, 109, 2n2222, lm317, Diodes, Emi Tools.





Mathematics (Lab-2)

Applied Mathematics Lab is managed by the Department of Applied Mathematics of the Faculty of New Technologies and is used for laboratory classes and research, personal study.

Specifications
Mathematics Lab is equipped with 21 computers, video projector and marker board. There is 1 server and 20 client computers running Windows 7 with various network-based software development programs such as Matlab, Maple, Java, Delphi, Visual C, Python, etc.





Networking Lab (Lab-3)

Networking Lab is managed by the Department of Computer Engineering of the Faculty of New Technologies and is used for laboratory classes and research, personal study.

Specifications
Networking Lab is equipped with 25 computers, video projector and marker board. There is 1 server and 24 client computers running Windows 7 with various network-based software development programs such as Eclipse, Delphi, VMware, Visual Studio, etc.





Software Development Lab (Lab-4)

Software Development Lab is managed by the Department of Computer Engineering of the Faculty of New Technologies and is used for laboratory classes and research, personal study.

Specifications
It is equipped with 25 computers, projector and marker board. All machines run Linux operating system, Fedora. Students mostly spend time programming. There is 1 server and 24 client computers with all pre-compiled software that comes with the OS itself.





Software Development Lab (Lab-5)

Software Development Lab is used by the Department of Computer Engineering, Faculty of New Technologies, and is intended for laboratory classes, research and personal study.

Specifications
Software Development Lab is equipped with 39 computers, video projector, marker board. All computers run Linux operating system, Fedora. Students spend more time programming. The lab has 1 server, 38 client computers, with all compiled programs that are provided by the operating system (OS) itself.





Electronics Lab (Lab-6)

Electronics Lab is managed by the Department of Industrial Electronics of the Faculty of New Technologies and is used for laboratory classes and research, personal study.

Specifications
Electronics Lab is equipped with 9 computers, video projector and marker board.
The lab supports more advanced experiments in signals and systems, communications, electromagnetism, and photonics. There are 31 stations in the Electronics Lab. Each station consists of:

• Lenovo ThinkCentre workstation running Windows 7
• Agilent Multimeter 34410A
• Agilent triple DC power supply E3631A
• Agilent Function Generator 33120A
• Oscilloscopes
• 15 stations include HP/Agilent MSO6012A mixed signal oscilloscope
• 16 stations include LeCroy WaveSurfer 422 touch-screen oscilloscope
• All devices on each station are connected to the computer



Smart Class

Smart Class is managed by the Department of Computer Science of the Faculty of New Technologies, used for distance learning, online seminars, collaborative courses and teleconferences.

Specifications
Smart Class is equipped with 1 laptop, 1 smart board, short-distance projector, LED TV, Web IP Camera for teleconferences, microphones, and sound system.
