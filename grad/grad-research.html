Competition for Supporting Graduate Students, PhD Candidates and Doctoral Students

DAAD Project "Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics (KGFI)"

Within the framework of the DAAD project "Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics", implemented from 2016 to 2019, a competition is announced among teachers and young scientists of Ala-Too International University and KSUCTA to conduct research at the Kyrgyz-German Faculty of Applied Informatics and West Saxon University of Zwickau (WHZ).

Aim

Goal - to attract scientific research into teaching and strengthen the teaching staff at KGFI. The criterion for participation in the program is the relevance of the dissertation topic. Additionally, the defense of the candidate's dissertation is planned no later than two years. Research and teaching are conducted at KGFI. The candidate commits to teaching and conducting research at KGFI for at least three years.

Research Areas:

Within the project, research activities in the following areas are supported:

• Information Security

• Applied Informatics - Mobile Systems

Supervision of research topics is conducted by the Dean of KGFI Faculty, Dr. Abdullaev A.A.

Support Form:

• Honorarium for conducting scientific research at KGFI in the amount of 250.00€ per month. At the same time, 50% of the workload should be allocated for scientific research;

• Research internship lasting up to 3 months at the University of Zwickau. Travel expenses up to 700€ are covered by the project and a monthly scholarship of 1,000€ is provided. Health insurance in Germany must also be purchased from this amount.

Required Documents (in English or German):

  • • Completed application form;

  • • Detailed written exposition (Exposè) on the dissertation topic;

  • • Recommendation letter from the main supervisor;

  • • Curriculum Vitae;

  • • Certificate confirming knowledge of German or English at level B1.

Selection Committee:

  • • Dean of KGFI, Dr. Abdullaev A.A.;

  • • DAAD Project Manager.

Application Deadlines:

  • • No later than August 31, 2024;

  • • Send to: kgfi.project@gmail.com

Selection results will be announced on September 15, 2024.

For questions contact: kgfi.project@gmail.com
