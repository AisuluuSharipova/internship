Admission Requirements

a) Major Requirements:

Bachelor's or Specialist's degree in Computer Science with a GPA of 75% or higher for the last two years of study. Bachelor's or Specialist's degree in technical fields with a GPA of 85% or higher in computer science-related subjects for the last two years of study is also accepted. These competencies must be documented. Professional experience in programming may be recognized as meeting the admission criteria. If necessary, the student must take advanced programming and software development courses in the undergraduate program of AIU or partner universities. These required courses are not offered in the evening or on weekends. Additional fees may apply for these courses beyond the main tuition fee.


b) Foreign Language Requirements:

▪ English language level B2 or

▪ English language level B1 or German language level B2

English language proficiency is determined by Ala-Too International University instructors.

German language proficiency is determined by partner university instructors.

List of documents required for admission

Documents for Master's program admission

  1. 1. Application form

  2. 2. Original Bachelor's (Specialist's) diploma with transcript

  3. 3. Copy of diploma supplement

  4. 4. 2 photos (3x4 cm)

  5. 5. Copy of passport (original for verification)

  6. 6. Contract

  7. 7. Personal record sheet

Note for international students:

Important information for foreign citizens

Translation of passport, diploma and its supplement into Kyrgyz or Russian languages (translation must be certified by a certified notary or translation companies).
